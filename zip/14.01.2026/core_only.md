### 1.2 Quartic Landau Potential

$$V(u) = \frac{a}{2}u^2 + \frac{\delta}{4}u^4 - su$$

$$\frac{d\Omega}{dt} = -\int_\Omega \left[ M_C |\mu_C|^2 + M_I |\mu_I|^2 \right] d\mathbf{x} \leq 0$$
