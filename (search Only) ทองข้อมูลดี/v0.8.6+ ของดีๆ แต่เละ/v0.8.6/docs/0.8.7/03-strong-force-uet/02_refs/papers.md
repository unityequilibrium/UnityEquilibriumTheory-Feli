# Strong Force: Key References

## Foundational Papers

### Yukawa Theory
- **Yukawa, H.** (1935). "On the Interaction of Elementary Particles"
  - Proc. Phys. Math. Soc. Japan 17, 48-57
  - First prediction of meson exchange

### Quark Model
- **Gell-Mann, M.** (1964). "A Schematic Model of Baryons and Mesons"
  - Physics Letters 8, 214-215
  - DOI: 10.1016/S0031-9163(64)92001-3

- **Zweig, G.** (1964). "An SU(3) Model for Strong Interaction Symmetry"
  - CERN preprint 8182/TH.401

### Quantum Chromodynamics
- **Gross, D.J. & Wilczek, F.** (1973). "Ultraviolet Behavior of Non-Abelian Gauge Theories"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 30, 1343
  - [Nobel Prize 2004]

- **Politzer, H.D.** (1973). "Reliable Perturbative Results for Strong Interactions"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 30, 1346

### Confinement
- **Wilson, K.G.** (1974). "Confinement of Quarks"
  - Phys. Rev. D 10, 2445
  - DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.10.2445

## Lattice QCD
- **Creutz, M.** (1980). "Monte Carlo Study of Quantized SU(2) Gauge Theory"
  - Phys. Rev. D 21, 2308

- **FLAG Collaboration** (2021). "FLAG Review 2021"
  - Eur. Phys. J. C 82, 869
  - [arXiv:2111.09849](https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.09849)

## Cornell Potential
- **Eichten, E. et al.** (1978). "Charmonium: The Model"
  - Phys. Rev. D 17, 3090
  - V(r) = -4αs/(3r) + σr

## Reviews
- **Brambilla, N. et al.** (2014). "QCD and Strongly Coupled Gauge Theories"
  - Eur. Phys. J. C 74, 2981
  - [arXiv:1404.3723](https://arxiv.org/abs/1404.3723)
