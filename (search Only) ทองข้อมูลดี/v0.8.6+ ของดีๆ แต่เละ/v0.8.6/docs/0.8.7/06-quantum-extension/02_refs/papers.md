# Quantum Extension: Key References

## Foundations of Quantum Mechanics

### Origins
- **Planck, M.** (1900). "Über das Gesetz der Energieverteilung im Normalspectrum"
  - Ann. Phys. 4, 553-563
  - E = hν quantization

- **Heisenberg, W.** (1927). "Über den anschaulichen Inhalt der quantentheoretischen Kinematik"
  - Z. Phys. 43, 172-198
  - Uncertainty principle

- **Schrödinger, E.** (1926). "Quantisierung als Eigenwertproblem"
  - Ann. Phys. 79, 361-376

## Casimir Effect
- **Casimir, H.B.G.** (1948). "On the Attraction Between Two Conducting Plates"
  - Proc. K. Ned. Akad. Wet. 51, 793-795

- **Lamoreaux, S.K.** (1997). "Demonstration of the Casimir Force"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 78, 5-8
  - [First precision measurement]

- **Mohideen, U. & Roy, A.** (1998). "Precision Measurement of the Casimir Force"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 81, 4549
  - [arXiv:physics/9805038](https://arxiv.org/abs/physics/9805038)

## Stochastic Electrodynamics (SED)
- **Boyer, T.H.** (1975). "Random Electrodynamics: Casimir Effect"
  - Phys. Rev. D 11, 790
  - [Classical derivation of Casimir]

- **Marshall, T.W.** (1963). "Random Electrodynamics"
  - Proc. R. Soc. A 276, 475

- **de la Peña, L. & Cetto, A.M.** (1996). "The Quantum Dice"
  - Springer, ISBN: 978-0792338185
  - [SED textbook]

## Vacuum Energy Problem
- **Weinberg, S.** (1989). "The Cosmological Constant Problem"
  - Rev. Mod. Phys. 61, 1-23
  - [10^120 discrepancy]

## Quantum Tunneling
- **Gamow, G.** (1928). "Zur Quantentheorie des Atomkernes"
  - Z. Phys. 51, 204-212
  - [Alpha decay tunneling]

## Reviews
- **Milonni, P.W.** (1994). "The Quantum Vacuum"
  - Academic Press
  - ISBN: 978-0124980808
