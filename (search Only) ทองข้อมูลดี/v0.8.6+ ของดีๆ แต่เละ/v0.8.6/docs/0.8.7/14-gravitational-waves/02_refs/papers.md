# Gravitational Waves: Key References

## Foundational Papers

### Einstein's Prediction
- **Einstein, A.** (1916). "Näherungsweise Integration der Feldgleichungen..."
  - Sitzungsber. Preuss. Akad. Wiss. Berlin, 688-696
  - [First prediction of GW]

- **Einstein, A.** (1918). "Über Gravitationswellen"
  - Sitzungsber. Preuss. Akad. Wiss. Berlin, 154-167

### Quadrupole Formula
- **Landau, L.D. & Lifshitz, E.M.** (1951). *Classical Theory of Fields*
  - Chapter on gravitational radiation

## LIGO Detection

### First Detection
- **Abbott, B.P. et al. (LIGO)** (2016). "Observation of Gravitational Waves from a Binary Black Hole Merger"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 116, 061102
  - [arXiv:1602.03837](https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.03837)
  - [Nobel Prize 2017]

### Multi-messenger Astronomy
- **Abbott, B.P. et al.** (2017). "GW170817: Observation of Gravitational Waves from a Binary Neutron Star Inspiral"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 119, 161101
  - [arXiv:1710.05832](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.05832)
