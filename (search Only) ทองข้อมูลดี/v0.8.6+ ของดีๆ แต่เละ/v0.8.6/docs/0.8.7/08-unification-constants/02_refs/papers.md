# Fundamental Constants: Key References

## Fine Structure Constant

### Discovery
- **Sommerfeld, A.** (1916). "Zur Quantentheorie der Spektrallinien"
  - Ann. Phys. 51, 1-94
  - α ≈ 1/137 first identified

### Precision Measurements
- **Gabrielse, G. et al.** (2006). "New Determination of Fine Structure Constant"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 97, 030802
  - [arXiv:hep-ex/0604007](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-ex/0604007)

- **Parker, R.H. et al.** (2018). "Measurement of Fine Structure Constant"
  - Science 360, 191-195
  - [Most precise: 0.2 ppb]

### CODATA
- **Mohr, P.J. et al.** (2016). "CODATA Recommended Values"
  - Rev. Mod. Phys. 88, 035009
  - [arXiv:1507.07956](https://arxiv.org/abs/1507.07956)
