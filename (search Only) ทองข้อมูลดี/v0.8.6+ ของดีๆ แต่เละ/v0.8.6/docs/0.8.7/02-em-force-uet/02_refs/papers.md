# Electromagnetic Force: Key References

## Foundational Papers

### Classical Electromagnetism
- **Coulomb, C.A.** (1785). "Premier Mémoire sur l'Électricité et le Magnétisme"
  - Mémoires de l'Académie Royale des Sciences

- **Maxwell, J.C.** (1865). "A Dynamical Theory of the Electromagnetic Field"
  - Phil. Trans. R. Soc. 155, 459-512
  - DOI: 10.1098/rstl.1865.0008

### Quantum Electrodynamics
- **Feynman, R.P.** (1948). "Space-Time Approach to Quantum Electrodynamics"
  - Phys. Rev. 76, 769
  - DOI: 10.1103/PhysRev.76.769

- **Schwinger, J.** (1948). "On Quantum-Electrodynamics and the Magnetic Moment of the Electron"
  - Phys. Rev. 73, 416
  - DOI: 10.1103/PhysRev.73.416

## Geometric/Emergent EM
- **Kaluza, T.** (1921). "Zum Unitätsproblem der Physik"
  - Sitzungsber. Preuss. Akad. Wiss. Berlin, 966-972

- **Klein, O.** (1926). "Quantentheorie und fünfdimensionale Relativitätstheorie"
  - Z. Phys. 37, 895-906

- **Lindgren, J. & Liukkonen, J.** (2019). "Electric Charge as a Form of Imaginary Energy"
  - [arXiv:1903.00066](https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.00066)

## Vacuum Energy
- **Casimir, H.B.G.** (1948). "On the Attraction Between Two Perfectly Conducting Plates"
  - Proc. K. Ned. Akad. Wet. 51, 793-795

- **Lamoreaux, S.K.** (1997). "Demonstration of the Casimir Force"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 78, 5
  - DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.78.5

## Precision Tests
- **Gabrielse, G. et al.** (2006). "New Determination of the Fine Structure Constant"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 97, 030802
  - [arXiv:hep-ex/0604007](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-ex/0604007)
