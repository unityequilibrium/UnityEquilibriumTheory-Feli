# Weak Force: Key References

## Foundational Papers

### Beta Decay Theory
- **Fermi, E.** (1934). "Versuch einer Theorie der β-Strahlen"
  - Z. Phys. 88, 161-177
  - Four-fermion interaction

- **Pauli, W.** (1930). Letter predicting neutrino
  - "Dear radioactive ladies and gentlemen..."

### Parity Violation
- **Lee, T.D. & Yang, C.N.** (1956). "Question of Parity Conservation in Weak Interactions"
  - Phys. Rev. 104, 254
  - [Nobel Prize 1957]

- **Wu, C.S. et al.** (1957). "Experimental Test of Parity Conservation in Beta Decay"
  - Phys. Rev. 105, 1413

### Electroweak Unification
- **Glashow, S.L.** (1961). "Partial-symmetries of Weak Interactions"
  - Nucl. Phys. 22, 579-588

- **Weinberg, S.** (1967). "A Model of Leptons"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 19, 1264
  - [Nobel Prize 1979]

- **Salam, A.** (1968). "Weak and Electromagnetic Interactions"
  - Proc. Nobel Symposium 8, 367

### Higgs Mechanism
- **Higgs, P.W.** (1964). "Broken Symmetries and the Masses of Gauge Bosons"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 13, 508
  - [Nobel Prize 2013]

- **Englert, F. & Brout, R.** (1964). "Broken Symmetry and Mass of Gauge Particles"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 13, 321

## W/Z Discovery
- **UA1 Collaboration** (1983). "Experimental observation of the W boson"
  - Phys. Lett. B 122, 103

- **UA2 Collaboration** (1983). "Observation of the Z⁰ boson"
  - Phys. Lett. B 129, 130

## Precision Tests
- **ALEPH, DELPHI, L3, OPAL, LEP Collaborations** (2006). "Precision Electroweak Measurements"
  - Phys. Rep. 427, 257
  - [arXiv:hep-ex/0509008](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-ex/0509008)

## CKM Matrix
- **Cabibbo, N.** (1963). "Unitary Symmetry and Leptonic Decays"
  - Phys. Rev. Lett. 10, 531

- **Kobayashi, M. & Maskawa, T.** (1973). "CP-Violation in the Renormalizable Theory"
  - Prog. Theor. Phys. 49, 652
  - [Nobel Prize 2008]
