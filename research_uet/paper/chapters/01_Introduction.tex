\section{The Broken Standard}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Gravity:} General Relativity is incompatible with Quantum Field Theory at the Planck scale.
    \item \textbf{Dark Matter:} 85\% of the universe's mass is invisible and composed of unknown particles.
    \item \textbf{Dark Energy:} The expansion of the universe is accelerating, requiring a cosmological constant $\Lambda$ that differs from vacuum energy predictions by $10^{120}$ orders of magnitude.
    \item \textbf{Neutrino Mass:} The Standard Model predicts massless neutrinos, contradicting oscillation data.
\end{itemize}

Physicists have resorted to "Shadow Math"---adding arbitrary parameters (inflatons, dark sectors, supersymmetry) to fit data without explanatory power.
