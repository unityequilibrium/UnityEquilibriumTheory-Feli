> **Topic 0.30**: Strategic Safety (Preveting "Plane vs Tree" Collisions)

## 1. The Collision Problem
In a city where trees reach 1km+ altitude (Mega-Flora), traditional aviation must evolve. We cannot rely on pilots "seeing" the tree; we need the tree to "talk" to the plane.

## 2. Technical Mechanisms (Safety Layers)

### A. Radar Visibility (Passive Mirror)
*   **Mechanism:** Foliage in the Crown (Level 3) is genetically programmed to deposit a thin layer of **Graphene** (Topic 0.28 synergy) on the leaf surface.
*   **Result:** The tree crown becomes a highly reflective radar surface. It appears on aircraft radar as a solid, permanent obstacle equivalent to a mountain or building.

### B. Bio-Transponders (Radio-Frequency Sap)
*   **Mechanism:** The tree's vascular system (Xylem/Phloem) is modified to transport ionic compounds that create **Electromagnetic Resonance** at 1030/1090 MHz (Standard SSR frequencies).
*   **Result:** The tree literally "squawks" its presence to the TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) of nearby aircraft. The plane's computer sees the tree as an "Authorized Obstacle."

### C. Bio-Lighthouses (Night Safety)
*   **Mechanism:** Expression of high-intensity **Luciferase** proteins in "Sentinel Branches" at the highest peaks.
*   **Energy Source:** Sugar surplus from the Mycelial Network (Topic 0.30/Engine 2).
*   **Result:** Year-round, self-powering red/orange bioluminescent beacons that mark the tree's height for visual navigation.

## 3. The "Sky Port" Interface
*   **VTOL Docking:** The trees sprout flat, reinforced circular platforms that act as "Charging/Landing Pads."
*   **Autonomous Avoidance:** All UET-certified aircraft must have an "Eco-Pilot" module that interfaces with the Mycelial Network's signaling, creating a 500m "No-Go Zone" around the main trunks unless docking.

**Conclusion:** We don't need runways. The city is the runway, and the trees are the controllers.
